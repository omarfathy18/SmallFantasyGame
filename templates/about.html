To play

You have to create an account first then log in to get the access to play the egyptian league fantasy AND the premier league fantasy.


What is fantasy?

It is a game where you choose 11 players from the (egyptian OR premier) league from different teams (alahly & zamalek) OR (arsenal & chelsea & liverpool & man city & man united) in different positions.
After each match the player gets points according to his performance.
After each week you get the total points from the players you have.
Try to get points more than other teams.


How to play?

Choose a league
Go to "pick team"
Choose 11 players
Wait for the week to end
Go to "points"
See your total points and the average points and the highest points.


Rules

There is a deadline before each week start then you can't change anything in the team.
Each week you have to choose a captain for the team.
The captain get points *2
